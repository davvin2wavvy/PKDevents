Welcome to my world of event planning! My passion for crafting unforgettable experiences began in childhood, where I loved decorating birthday parties for my kids. As I grew older, I recognized that every occasion—big or small—deserves to be celebrated, from life’s milestones to everyday moments. Friends often encouraged me to become an event coordinator, seeing my enthusiasm for making life fun and memorable.

My journey started when I planned a college send-off party, followed by a heartfelt 90th birthday celebration. These experiences allowed me to unleash my creativity and truly understand my clients' visions. I thrive on transforming dreams into reality, celebrating all of life’s occasions, and making each moment worth living. Let’s work together to make your special moments truly extraordinary!
